About Sweden

Sweden is an oblong country placed in the north, it has a lot of forests, meatballs, ice-hockey and football.

Fika

In Sweden, people like to have a coffee. A Fika is when at least two people go out for a coffee, but you need to sit down for at least 30 minutes and eat a pastry, a cinnamon roll or a biscuit.

Lagom

Lagom is a word that the sweds absolutely loves! They use it all the time and it means ”just enough”, so when someone askes ”how much food do you want?” The answer is always lagom! Not to much, not to little, just enough.
